Here are some of the most common questions businesses ask before starting their Google Ads journey.

What is the minimum Google Ads budget?

We recommend starting with at least ₹20,000–₹30,000 per month depending on your industry and competition.

Most campaigns are launched within 3–7 business days after receiving all required business information.

Yes. Your Google Ads account remains completely under your ownership and you have full access at all times.

Do you provide monthly performance reports?

Yes. Every client receives detailed reports covering conversions, clicks, ROAS, CPA and overall campaign growth.

Can you improve an existing Google Ads account?

Absolutely. We regularly audit and optimize existing campaigns to improve performance while reducing wasted spend.
